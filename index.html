meet sarah

Hi! I'm Sarah Creasman. I'm an aspiring front end developer. I got my start in programming with HTML and CSS, learning the basics to create templates for a niche writing community online. The hobby grew and I found myself wanting more and more challenging codes. I began to challenge myself until one day, my husband asked why I didn't do something like this for a living. A few years later, I was enrolled at Jefferson Community and Technical College in their Computer Information Technology program, focusing on computer programming. Upon graduation, I began Code Louisville to further my skills in hopes of making this into a career.

Feel free to look around and view my portfolios. I have codes and graphic design samples featured that I'm proud of. I appreciate you taking a look! Feel free to contact me with any questions or comments.
